About Us

UC San Diego

The Egyptian Student Association at UC San Diego is dedicated to promoting Egyptian culture, heritage, and community on campus. We aim to create a welcoming environment for all students interested in Egyptian culture and provide opportunities for cultural exchange and learning.

Our organization hosts various events throughout the year, including cultural celebrations, educational workshops, and social gatherings. We welcome students of all backgrounds to join us in celebrating the rich history and traditions of Egypt.
